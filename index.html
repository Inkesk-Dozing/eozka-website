Origin

A conversation.
Then two products.
Then everything else.

Four college friends decided the gap between what students could build and what they were actually being asked to do was too large to ignore. eOzka started as that decision.

The name comes from OSKA — a word coined to capture what the organisation is for. A structure that holds ambition without letting it collapse under its own weight.
